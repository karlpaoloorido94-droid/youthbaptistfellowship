About YBF

Youth Bible Fellowship (YBF) is a vibrant community dedicated to nurturing young people in their faith journey through the teachings of the Bible.

Our Mission

To grow young people spiritually through the Word of God, fostering a deep relationship with Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

A Christ-centered, Bible-rooted youth ministry that equips the next generation to live out their faith boldly.
